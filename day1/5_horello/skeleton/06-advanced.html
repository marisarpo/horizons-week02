The platform that gives you a shared horizon.

It's high time you stopped digging through emails and Slack to find that message from your housemate. Communicate, upload files, add due dates, and plot to #DominateAllTheHouses, all within a Horello board.

Sign Up - It's Free.

This is a Horello board. It's a list of lists filled with cards, used by your house or by yourself.

Drag and drop cards between lists to show progress. Add as many people as you need and drag them to cards. Add and reorder lists as you need. Horello adapts to your project, house, and workflow.

You’ll see everything about your project just by glancing at the board, and it all updates in real-time. There’s nothing to set up and everyone gets it instantly.

Horello will never overwhelm you with features you won’t use. For those who want more out of their boards, there are Power-Ups like calendar, card aging, and voting, that you can turn on. It’s a way to offer more without cluttering things for everyone.

Horello stays perfectly in sync across all your devices, wherever you are. There are fast and intuitive apps for the web, Android phones and tablets, iPhone and iPad and Kindle Fire.
